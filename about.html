Our Story

Arlo's Pet Care was born from a simple belief: every pet deserves love, attention, and professional care while their humans are away. Founded in 2018 by animal lover Arlo Martinez, what started as dog walking for neighbors has grown into the most trusted pet care service in the Metro Area.

Our journey began when Arlo noticed a gap in the market for truly personalized, professional pet care. Too many services treated pets like numbers, rushing through visits without genuine connection. Arlo knew there was a better way ‚Äì one that prioritized the unique needs and personality of each furry client.

Today, we're proud to serve over 200 families with a dedicated team of certified pet care professionals. Every member of our team shares Arlo's original vision: treating every pet as if they were our own. From daily walks to extended boarding stays, we bring the same level of care, attention, and love that we give to our own pets.

What sets us apart isn't just our certifications or insurance (though we have both!) ‚Äì it's our genuine love for animals and commitment to building lasting relationships with both pets and their families. When you choose Arlo's Pet Care, you're not just hiring a service; you're joining a community of pet lovers who understand that your furry family member deserves nothing but the best.
