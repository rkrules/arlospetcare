About Arlo's Pet Care

Where passion meets professional pet care excellence

Our Story

Here at Arlo's we know that your pets are family. That's why we strive to maintain compassionate and reliable care individually tailored to your pets needs. And because we know no two pets are the same, we pride ourselves in working with our pet parents to make sure they are supported in enriching the lives of their pets.

Founded and operated by West Hartford resident and animal lover Jennifer Zilinyi, the goal at Arlos Petcare Services LLC has always been clear "be the village".

"When I started Arlo's, I had no idea how essential the support aspect would become. With so many pet parents working longer days and spending time at their children's extra curricular activities it became clear that I was in a position to add relief to the day. I could help a family's day flow a little smoother. And not just parents! People caring for elderly family members, people who are empty nesters looking to engage in activities they love, and those who were looking to travel again. My goal became clear, be the village that so many of us need. Adding peace of mind with excellent care and communication. This has become one of the greatest gifts in my life."

Arlo's offers a variety of services to suit your family's needs and look forward to offering future services including dog training!
