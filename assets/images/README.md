# Images Directory

Place your custom images here:

- **Logo**: Your business logo (PNG or SVG recommended)
- **Hero Images**: High-quality photos of happy pets
- **Team Photos**: Professional headshots of your team members
- **Service Photos**: Images representing each service
- **Testimonial Photos**: Photos of pets/clients (with permission)

## Image Guidelines

- **Format**: JPG for photos, PNG for graphics with transparency, SVG for logos
- **Size**: Optimize images for web (compress before uploading)
- **Dimensions**: 
  - Hero images: 1200x800px minimum
  - Team photos: 400x400px square
  - Service icons: 200x200px
- **File naming**: Use descriptive names (e.g., `golden-retriever-walking.jpg`)

## Free Image Resources

While developing, you can use:
- [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com) - Free high-quality photos
- [Pexels](https://pexels.com) - Free stock photos
- [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com) - Free images

**Note**: Replace all placeholder images with your actual business photos before launching!
