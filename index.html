Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

My pet is very anxious and often times can't be left alone, could I book you hourly?

Absolutely! We understand that some pets have a hard time being alone and are happy to offer an hourly rate so that you can enjoy your time out while your pet is lovingly cared for.

Do you offer gift certificates?

We sure do! You can gift anything from walks to any of our pet sitting packages. We couldn't think of a better gift!

Are there cancellation fees?

Sometimes. We understand that life is unpredictable. While for walks, a cancellation fee may not apply, for pet sitting if booked for a long trip there may be a fee of half expected pay for last minute cancellation.

What is the cancellation time frame?

For scheduled walks, day of, but for cancelled trips we would need 72 or more hours in advance depending on length of trip.

I have a pet that comes from a traumatic background, do you work with animals that may need a little more time to get comfortable?

Sure do! Our pets don't get to determine where their life starts, at Arlo's we pride ourselves on our compassionate care and understand that some of our animals need more time and patience. This is our owners specialty!

I feel kind of weird letting a stranger into my home, is there something I can do to help ease my nerves?

Yes! At Arlo's we value transparency, in home cameras are preferred. This allows pet parents and walkers/sitters to keep good communication about your pet's needs.
